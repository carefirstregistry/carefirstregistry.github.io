Get affordable in-home nursing care

Care First Registry provides qualified, reliable in-home nursing for seniors and the disabled

Would you or your loved one prefer to stay living in their own home instead of transitioning to a nursing home or assisted living facility? As a domestic referral agency, we help connect seniors with screened RNs and LVNs experienced in providing professional care with non-medical, in-home care services, at an affordable rate.

Hire nurses quickly and easily

Care First Registry thoroughly screens and interviews all of our RNs and LVNs. All the domestic workers we employ are Certified by the State of California as a Certified Caregiver and have been fingerprinted and background checked.

Our Services

Registured Nurse (RN)

  • Total patient assessments and interventions
  • Administer medication
  • Start IV and maintain ordered IV meds/hydration
  • Place and maintain Foley catheters and G-tubes, trach & vent care, wound care and colostomy

Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN)

  • Patient education
  • Administer medication
  • Monitor and provide intervention
  • Monitor, care for and maintain Foley catheters, trachs, vents, G-tubes, blood draws and other external to internal devices, wound care and colostomy.

Caregiver / Certified Nurse Assistance (CNA)

Primary Activities of Daily Living

  • Dressing
  • Meals
  • Kitchen Upkeep
  • Transferring
  • Bathing
  • Toiletting

Instrumental Activites of Daily Living

  • Medication Reminders
  • Light Housekeeping
  • Laundry
  • Cognitive Stimulation
  • Assistance with hobbies
  • Everyday Transportation

Serving seniors, mentally and physically disabled clients throughout LA county
